Pesto Pasta with Chicken Recipe

Ingrediants :

What you need to make 8 servings
    • 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta
    • 1 teaspoon olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
    • ½ cup pesto sauce
    • ⅓ cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips

Get ready to start cooking

Instuctions

Step 1 :

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2 :

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.

Step 3 :

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Sauté garlic until tender.

Step 4 :

Stir in chicken and season with red pepper flakes.
Cook until chicken is golden and cooked through.

Step 5 :

Combine pasta, chicken, pesto, and sun-dried tomatoes in a large bowl;
toss to coat evenly.

Step 6 :

EAT.
