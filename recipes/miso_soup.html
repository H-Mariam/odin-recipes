Miso Soup Recipe

Ingrediants :

What you need to make 4 servings
    • 4 cups water
    • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
    • 3 tablespoons miso paste
    • 1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
    • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Get ready to start cooking

Instructions

Step 1 :

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2 :

Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat;
bring to a boil.

Step 3 :

Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.

Step 4 :

Stir in tofu.

Step 5 :

Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup.
Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
