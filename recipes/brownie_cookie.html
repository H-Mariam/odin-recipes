Cookie/Brownie Recipe

Ingrediants :

What you need to make 24 servings
    • 1 (19.8 ounce) package brownie mix
    • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
    • ¼ cup vegetable oil
    • 2 eggs
    • 3 tablespoons water
    • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Get ready to start cooking

Instuctions

Step 1 :

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Lightly grease a baking sheet.

Step 2 :

Mix brownie mix, flour, oil, eggs, and water together in a large bowl;
Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet.

Step 3 :

Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are just set, about 9 minutes.
Let cool on baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 4 :

Stir in chicken and season with red pepper flakes.
Cook until chicken is golden and cooked through.

Step 5 :

Get your hot coco milk and enjoy
